Tzatziki

Tzatziki is a typical greek sauce which is great alongside with any type of meat.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cucumber
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 dl Greek yogurt
  • 1 - 2 large cloves of garlic
  • 1 tsp dried or fresh oregano (1 tsp dried equals 2 tbsp fresh)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Do this

  1. Rinse the cucumber and grate it on the coarsest side of a grater. Place in a sieve and mix in salt. Leave to drain for 30 minutes. Squeeze out the liquid with your hand.
  2. Stir together the yogurt, pressed garlic, cucumber seeds, oregano and olive oil. Season with black pepper
