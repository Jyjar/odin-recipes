Scones

Scones are a very tasty and easy to make bread.

Ingredients

  • 4 dl wheat
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 2 ml salt
  • 50 g butter
  • 2 dl milk
  1. Start the oven on 250Â°C.
  2. Mix wheat, baking powder and salt in a bowl. Add in butter and stir until the butter is disolved into the mixture. Add milk and stir quickly into a sticky dough.
  3. Make into 8 buns and add them to a baking sheet with baking paper. Bake in the middle of the oven for about 10-12 minutes.
  4. Serve with butter and cheese or jam and cream cheese.
